## Notes

- All audio is played client-side via `<audio>` elements from `/audio/…`.
- Chimes fire on phase transitions when "Play chime at phase transitions" is
  on in the focus prefs.
- The `None (silence)` soundtrack option in the UI does not correspond to a
  file — it disables music entirely.
